This important question is answered by the sampling theorem, which sates that a suitable Δ to make Eq. (28) exact is given by Δ ≤ 1∕(2fc), where fc = Nc∕T is the largest frequency contained in h(t) (i.e, cn is zero for |n|≥ Nc).
