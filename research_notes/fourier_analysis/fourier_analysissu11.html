We need a fast method for computing the above DST. All fast methods ﬁnally need to make use of the fast method used in the computation of DFT, i.e., FFT. To make use of FFT, we need to deﬁne the DST in a way that the DST can be easily connected to the DFT so that FFT can be easily applied. A standard way of making this easy is to deﬁne the DST via the DFT of an odd extension of the original data. Next, let us discuss this.
