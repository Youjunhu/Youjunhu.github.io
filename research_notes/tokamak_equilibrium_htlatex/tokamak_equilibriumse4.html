4 Curvilinear coordinate system

In many studies of tokamak plasmas, one need construct a curvilinear coordinate system based on a given magnetic coﬁguration in order to make the problem amenable to analytical methods or numerical methods. Speciﬁcally, one of the coordinate surfaces of the constructed system will be required to coincide with magnetic surfaces. In addition, the magnetic ﬁeld lines on a magnetic surface may be required to have some simple property (e.g., being a straight line) via carefully choosing the other two angular coordinates. Next, let us discuss some general properties about coordinates transformation.
