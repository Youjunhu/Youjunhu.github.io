Abstract

The nonlinear gyrokinetic equation given in Frieman-Chen’s paper[3] is re-derived in this note, providing more details on the derivation. The aspects that are relevant to numerical implementation of the gyrokinetic model are also discussed, which are actually the emphasis of this note.
